Background

Hello everyone at DBC! My name is Perry Anderson. I am currently twenty-four years old. I graduated from Bard College last summer with a B.A. in Biology. I love bizarre biological and medical facts. My favorite museum is the Mütter Museum in Philadelphia, check it out if you have a chance. I was also attracted to Biology because at its core it is all about complex interacting systems. I have a highly analytical and logical mental approach to life, which allowed me to really engage with the material. After graduating I had a lot of difficulty finding an entry-level research position, and I became increasingly unsure if research was right for me. I was attracted to DBC because of the intensity, and because I believe the skills that are taught are highly marketable. I also believe that computers, like life, are all about the interaction of systems, so I think my mindset will serve me well in this environment.

In terms of my interests I am kind of a nerd. I love videogames, anime, manga, and graphic novels. I also play magic the gathering occasionally, but I am horrible at it. I'm also on Hearthstone, look for the username SuperPants if you want to challenge me. Don't blame me for the name, it's the random generator's fault.
