Pair Programming 101

Benefits to the Individual

  • Catch simple mistakes or omissions
  • Complementing programming practices and styles
  • Larger knowledge base
  • Mentoring
  • Productivity
  • Accountability

Production Benefits

  • More bug free product
  • Product can incorporate more techniques
  • Product can be more complex
  • Efficiency
  • Quicker production cycle
  • Greater ownership of end product

Driver and Navigator Pairing

The driver is responsible for writing the code and the navigator is responsible for leading the driver and defining the direction of the project. Division of labor in this way allows the driver to focus on writing good code, and frees the navigator to manage workflow and design.

Reflections

I think our pairing session went well. This was actually my second paring session, it was a very different experience working with someone from DBC watching my progress. We were able to adress the goals set out before us efficiently, and we both definitely learned new tricks from each other. I do feel the driver-navigator roles are a little tricky to maintain.
